IPL History

The IPL Was Developed In 2008 And The Currently Consists Of Eight Teams In Eight Cities Across India. The First IPL Sission Won By Rajastan Royal's, The History Of IPL Is 14 Years's,The IPL Was Developed By IPL Governing Council The IPL Is 20Overs Super Tournament.

MI History

The MI Is IPL Cricket Team They Founded In 24 January 2008, The Home Ground Is Waskhede Stadium, Mumbai And The MI Is First IPL Team That Won 5 Times IPL Cup(2013,2015,2017,2019,2020) In The League.

CSK History

The CSK Is IPL Cricket Team Founded In 2008 , The Home Ground Is M.A Chidambaram Stadium ,Chennai Captained By M.S Dhoni .The CSK Is Second Team Of IPL That's Won Four Times IPL Cup(2010,2011,2018,2021) In The League.

KKR History

The KKR Is A IPL Cricket Team That Founded In 24 January 2008, The Home Ground Of KKR In Eden Gardens,Kolkata The KKR Won Two Times In IPL Cup(2012,2014) In The League.

RR History

The RR Is A IPL Cricket Team That's Founded In 2008, The Home Ground Is Rawai Mansingh Stadium ,Jaipur. The Also Called Most Expensive Team In The IPL Beacause Of Bidding Was $67 millions In The League And RR Is Won The First IPL Sessions.

SRH History

The SRH Is A IPL Cricket Team That's Founded In 2012 Owned By Kalanithi Maran The SRH Only Won One Time IPL Cup(2016) In The League.

RCB History

The RCB Is A IPL Cricket Team That's Founded In 2008 By United Sprite's, The Home Ground Of RCB In M . Chinnswany Stadium. The RCB Not Won The IPL Cup In The League.

LSG History

The IPL 2022 Founded Two New Teams(Lucknow And Gujrat) And They IPL Teams Divded Into Two Group ,Group A And Group B.

DC History

The DC Is A IPL Cricket Team Founded In 2008 By GMR Group And JSW Group,Home Ground Of DC In Jaitley Stadium And The DC Not Won The IPL Cup In The League.
